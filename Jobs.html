Generic Job

Front-End Developer

Are you a front-end developer who boasts the front-end creativity to build a website that is as beautiful as it is effective? Can you define requirements, logic, and a framework to support the creative vision and custom build your own websites? If you're ready to produce stunning websites for large and small brands and innovative startups, you may be our next developer.

  • 3+ years of professional experience in software development
  • Good understanding of front-end technologies, including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery
  • Experience building user interfaces for websites and/or web applications
  • Experience designing and developing responsive design websites
  • Ability to convert comprehensive layout and wireframes into working HTML pages
  • Knowledge of how to interact with RESTful APIs and formats (JSON, XML)
  • Proficient understanding of code versioning tools
