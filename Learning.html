CodeCademy Review

CodeCademy is a learning platform with both free sections and paid sections that unblock in the form of a membership. It has a well design variety of career paths or specific skills to be learned, which makes it easy to select depending on your experience or for beginners. The information is mostly read by the user and then excercises in a three way divided screen to see what youre coding, altough it contains other forms as well like videos of walkthroughs and explenations. The best platform we have used for learning puposes specially for people that are looking to be self-taught.

Pros

  • Top-Tier content
  • Great value for the price
  • From zero to interview
  • Beginner friendly
  • Content for experienced SE
  • Excelent Design of the platform
  • Continuos Practice
  • Excellent community

Cons

  • Pay to unlock the best (Although it is worth it)
  • Self-Motivation is a most as you push yourself through
  • Not the typical Bootcamp / Classroom

Check it out for yourselves: CodeCademy / https://www.codecademy.com/
